About

Israeli Television Academy Awards

Award-winning Israeli sound designer, composer, and mixer with a passion for creating immersive sonic experiences.

Known for his work on Bad Boy (2025), winner of Best Drama Series at the Israeli Television Academy Awards, and Junam (2024), which won Best Fiction Film at the Cinema South Festival. He also contributed to Netflix's Cold Case: The Tylenol Murders (2025) and the award-winning short Malachi.

Formerly part of the team at SoundAround Studios, Matan has collaborated on a wide range of acclaimed film and television productions. His focus extends into interactive media — designing adaptive audio systems and composing music for the game industry.
